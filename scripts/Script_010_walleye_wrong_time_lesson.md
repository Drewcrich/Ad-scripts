# Script 010 ‚Äì Walleye: I Fished at the Wrong Time (Three-clip contrast video)

**Species:** Walleye  
**Style:** UGC confession/contrast story (three-clip stitched video)  
**Platforms:** TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels  
**Concept:** Concept 07 - UGC Fisherman Testimonial

**Creative Twist:** This script uses **visual and emotional contrast** to show the dramatic difference between fishing at the wrong time (slow, boring, frustrating) versus fishing during the actual bite window (action-packed, exciting). The confession format makes it more relatable and entertaining than typical "here's a tip" videos. The four-clip structure keeps dialogue manageable and ensures the AI transparency message doesn't get cut off.

**Note:** This script uses four clips (15s + 12s + 10s + 10s) that will be stitched together in CapCut for a 47-second total video.

## Performance notes

(To be filled in later in analytics/shorts-log.csv once view/like data is available.)

**Testing focus:** This script tests a **confession/contrast format** that should be more emotionally engaging than straight educational tips. The four-clip structure (mistake ‚Üí realization ‚Üí success ‚Üí transparency) creates narrative tension and payoff while keeping each clip's dialogue manageable.

**AI Acknowledgment:** Clip 4 is dedicated to AI transparency, keeping the message clear and preventing dialogue from being cut off. This approach:
1. Separates the success moment (Clip 3) from the transparency message (Clip 4)
2. Ensures the AI acknowledgment isn't rushed or cut off
3. Allows each clip to stay within the 10-15 second limit
4. Emphasizes that the PrimeBite data is 100% real

**Creative elements that make this unique:**
1. **Visual contrast:** Harsh afternoon light (wrong time) vs warm dawn light (right time)
2. **Emotional arc:** Frustration ‚Üí realization ‚Üí satisfaction
3. **Confession format:** More relatable than "expert giving tips"
4. **Before/after structure:** But with clearer cause-and-effect (timing) rather than just product testimonial
5. **Self-deprecating humor:** Makes the speaker more likable and authentic

**Key differentiators from other scripts:**
- Uses emotional storytelling instead of straightforward tips
- Shows both failure and success (contrast creates interest)
- More cinematic variety: three different times of day, four different emotional beats
- Confession format is more engaging than instruction format
- The "mistake" angle is more memorable and shareable
- Four-clip structure ensures AI transparency message is delivered clearly without rushing

**Predicted strengths:**
- Hook is strong (problem + curiosity gap: "wanna know why?")
- Contrast should maintain attention through all three clips
- Relatable mistake that many anglers have made
- Clear, visual proof of concept (no fish ‚Üí fish)
- Emotional payoff at the end

**Testing hypotheses:**
- Will confession/mistake format outperform straight tips?
- Does visual contrast (time of day) increase retention?
- Is "learning from failure" more engaging than "here's what works"?
