# Concept 03: Blueprint / Tech-Lab Explainers

**Theme:** "The Invisible Science of Fishing"  
**Visual Style:** High-tech, clean, analytical, data-driven overlays.  
**Goal:** To visualize the algorithm and data that makes PrimeBite work, appealing to the "analytical" angler.

---

## Core Idea
Most fishing content shows the *result* (the fish). This concept shows the *process* (the data). It visualizes the "invisible layer" of information that PrimeBite provides (moon phases, pressure, bite windows) as if it were a physical blueprint or a high-tech HUD.

---

## Visual Directions

### 1. The "X-Ray Vision" (HUD Overlay)
- **The Look:** Real fishing footage overlayed with futuristic wireframes, heat maps, and data points.
- **The Vibe:** "Iron Man" for fishing. Augmented Reality.
- **Key Elements:** Scanning lines, target reticles, scrolling data numbers.
- **Message:** "Stop guessing. Start seeing."

### 2. The "Lab Test" (Clean Room)
- **The Look:** A clean, white or dark grey "testing facility" or "blueprint table."
- **The Vibe:** Scientific, precise, premium.
- **Key Elements:** Holographic fish, moon phase sliders, mathematical formulas.
- **Message:** "We calculated 10,000 variables so you don't have to."

### 3. The "Data vs. Guessing" Split
- **The Look:** Split screen. Top is boring/gray (Guessing). Bottom is vibrant/tech-overlay (PrimeBite).
- **The Vibe:** Comparison, before/after.
- **Message:** "The difference isn't luck. It's data."
